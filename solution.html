Our SUPERPOWERED Problem


Climate change is beginning to cause potentially irreversible damage to our environment, with 1.2 trillion tons of polar ice melting each year due to rising temperatures. As stewards of the planet, it is our responsibility to future generations to act now and reduce our production of harmful CO2. We must take immediate action to capture energy from exhausted waste heat and direct it to purposes that would otherwise require electrical energy from the grid.


Introducing Ctrl-Z's Z-pipe!


The Z-Pipe recovers energy from waste heat generated by gas furnaces. It works by using thermoelectric generators or TEGs to create an electric current from hot furnace flue gasses that are normally just exhausted into the air. The Z-Pipe is made of several TEG modules that will be mounted in side openings on ULC certified, System 636 CPVC flue gas exhaust pipe, and connected in series.

The modules are connected to a voltage converter which can then be plugged into a mobile device for charging or a rechargeable battery for charging. For maximum temperature differential, and for safety reasons, the Z-Pipe is simply connected to your already-existing furnace exhaust pipe, outside the house.
