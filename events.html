Exchanging Ideas with FLL Team LEGO Legends 44851


On January 18th, 2022, we met with Team 44851 LEGO Legends.
Congratulations on a successful FLL season. Thank you for sharing your research project with us. Your secure package destination looks terrific and intriguing. We are very impressed by your creative presentation. We learned a lot from your innovative solution and wish you the best of luck in post-season competition.




Z-Box Presentation Posted on the Canadian Corrugated and Containerboard Association's YouTube Channel


On February 2nd, 2022, the Canadian Corrugated and Containerboard Association posted our FLL innovation project presentation for the world to see on their Youtube channel. Shortly after that, on February 8th, the board of directors of the CCCA watched our presentation at their quarterly board meeting.




Interviewed By FIRST Robotics Canada


On March 1st, 2022 we were interviewed by FIRST Robotics Canada on it's weekly FIRST Canada Livestream.
On the show, we shared our Z-Box innovation and our passion for STEM robotics, to robotics enthusiasts around the world.








Presenting Our Research Project at a School Assembly


On April 8, 2022, Ctrl-Z presented its research and innovative solution to 200 attendees from the Bayview Glen Lower School. The students were intrigued by our Z-Box sustainable packaging solution. It was a really great learning experience for both the audience and us.

Sharing with Fellow FIRST Robotics Enthusiasts at the FRC Ontario Provincial Championships


On April 16, 2022, Ctrl-Z Bayview Glen was invited to the FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) Provincial Championships held at the Paramount Foods Centre in Mississauga, ON. The event was an opportunity for the FRC competitors and fans to recognize Ctrl-Z for having recently won the FLL Ontario East Provincial Championship and to send the team off to the Houston FIRST Championship. CtrlZ demonstrated its robot to an audience of about 2200 at the FRC Provincials
